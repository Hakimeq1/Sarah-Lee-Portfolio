About

A partner leaders trust with people decisions

Sarah is a results-oriented HR Business Partner with eight years of experience across financial services and technology in Singapore. She has built a reputation for turning ambiguous, high-stakes people challenges — restructuring, hyper-growth, culture change — into structured, well-communicated programmes that leadership can act on.

At DBS Bank, she partners with four business divisions (roughly 600 staff) on talent planning, performance management, and leadership development, and has led a bank-wide employee experience initiative that lifted engagement scores by 14 points in 18 months. Earlier, at Grab, she helped scale the Singapore HR function through headcount growth from 200 to 450 employees.

"Comfortable working with senior leadership and frontline teams alike — HR that shows up in the room, not just in the policy."
  • Redesigned graduate recruitmentReduced time-to-hire by 22% and improved offer acceptance at DBS Bank.
  • Manager Effectiveness ProgrammeCo-designed and rolled out to 120 people managers at Grab.
  • Workday HRIS implementationLed rollout for the Singapore entity, training 30 HR and finance users.
